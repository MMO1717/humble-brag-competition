# 平台与关系风格 / Platform And Relationship Style

## English Prompt Notes

For workplace_channel, academic_forum, and public_social_media, keep the reply restrained and avoid exaggerated praise.

For close_friend in private_chat or group_chat, a light playful tone can work when the post itself is self-aware or joking.

For direct_message with acquaintance, classmate, or coworker, a grounded follow-up question is often safer than praise when the goal warns against sycophancy.

For stay_neutral or professional goals, prefer a neutral observation that acknowledges context without amplifying the flex.

Avoid calling out bragging directly in the response_text.

## 中文说明

平台、关系和互动目标会直接影响回复语气。公开、学术、职场场景要更克制。
